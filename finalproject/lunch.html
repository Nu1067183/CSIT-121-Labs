Quinoa Salad Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • Mixed greens
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Cucumber, sliced
  • Avocado, diced
  • Feta cheese, crumbled
  • Balsamic vinaigrette dressing

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, avocado, and feta cheese.
  2. Drizzle with balsamic vinaigrette dressing and toss to combine.
  3. Serve in a bowl and enjoy a refreshing quinoa salad!
